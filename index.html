About Yara Odeh

I'm Yara Odeh, a photographer based in Barcelona with a passion for telling stories through the lens. From the quiet intimacy of a couple's glance to the vibrant energy of a conference hall, I find beauty in every kind of moment.

My work spans portraits, weddings, fashion, couples, proposals, conferences, and events. Whether it's the joy of a proposal, the elegance of a bridal look, or the personality behind a portrait — I bring genuine care and artistic vision to every session.

I believe every person and every occasion deserves to be remembered beautifully. My goal is always the same: deliver images that make you feel something every time you look at them.
